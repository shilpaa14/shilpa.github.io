About Me

Hi, I'm Shilpa V Nayak, a Software Engineer based in India with a background in Computer Science. I have a strong interest in software development, cloud technologies, and networking, and I enjoy working across both system-level and application-level domains.

Currently, I work at Poornam Info Vision Private Limited, Kerala, where I work with Linux and Windows Server environments, troubleshoot application-level issues, and handle web hosting. I have hands-on experience with CPanel and Plesk, along with a solid understanding of networking concepts.

I also work with databases, focusing on efficient data management, performance optimization, and maintaining high availability for applications.

I am passionate about coding and software development. I enjoy building applications using Python, and I also have knowledge of Java and React. I love creating real-world projects, automating tasks, and continuously improving my development skills.

I am exploring modern technologies such as Cloud and AI to expand my expertise and stay up-to-date with the latest trends.

I completed my Bachelor of Engineering in Computer Science (2025) from St. Joseph Engineering College, Mangalore. During my studies, I developed projects including an ERP system and a mobile application, strengthening my problem-solving and development skills.

My goal is to grow as a Software Engineer and build scalable, efficient, and impactful applications while continuously learning new technologies.
